# Airbnb_Dashboard
# 📊 Global Airbnb Analytics Dashboard

## Business Problem

Airbnb operates across multiple cities with millions of listings and customer reviews. Understanding market performance, pricing strategies, customer engagement, and host trust through raw data is challenging and time-consuming.

The objective of this project was to transform complex data into an interactive dashboard that enables stakeholders to make faster and more informed business decisions.
## Project Goal
Develop a business intelligence dashboard that answers key business questions related to:

* Market performance across cities
* Listing growth over time
* Pricing by property type
* Customer review behaviour
* Host trust and verification
* Seasonal review trends

## Key Business Insights

* Paris, New York City, and Sydney contribute nearly half of the platform's listings and reviews.
* Entire homes generate the highest average prices compared to shared and private rooms.
* Almost 99% of customers write three or fewer reviews.
* Verified hosts receive significantly higher customer trust.
* Customer review activity follows strong seasonal trends.
